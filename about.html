Our Story

Illuminating Spaces Since Decades

NIA Global, in association with PSAT, has been at the forefront of India's lighting and automation industry for over 25 years. From humble beginnings, we have grown into one of the country's most trusted names in lighting solutions.

We are authorized representatives of 9+ world-class lighting and automation brands, giving our clients access to the latest technologies and innovations from across the globe. Our portfolio spans decorative, indoor, outdoor, architectural, underwater, and street lighting — along with smart automation systems.

With offices in New Delhi and Dehradun, we serve clients across India — from 5-star hotels and luxury resorts to premium residences, corporate offices, and large-scale commercial developments.
