01 — Designing

Lighting Design That
Transforms Spaces

Our design team works closely with architects, interior designers, and project managers to create lighting plans that perfectly balance form and function. We consider every detail — from ambient mood to task lighting, accent highlighting to energy efficiency.

Using the latest lighting simulation tools and 3D visualization software, we present detailed proposals that allow you to see exactly how your space will look before a single fixture is installed.

  • Site survey and analysis
  • 3D lighting visualization
  • Energy efficiency consulting
  • Custom fixture selection
02 — Manufacturing

Bespoke Fixtures
Crafted for You

From grand chandeliers to delicate pendant lights, our manufacturing division creates bespoke lighting fixtures that are as unique as the spaces they illuminate. Every piece is handcrafted by skilled artisans using premium materials.

We work with a variety of materials including crystal, brass, bronze, glass, and hand-blown glass to create pieces that range from classic elegance to bold contemporary designs.

  • Custom chandelier design and fabrication
  • Handcrafted decorative fixtures
  • Premium material sourcing
  • Quality control and testing
03 — Installation

Professional Installation
Done Right

Our team of certified technicians handles every aspect of the installation process — from receiving and inspecting fixtures to mounting, wiring, testing, and final commissioning. We follow strict safety protocols and industry best practices.

Whether it's a 20-foot chandelier in a hotel lobby or a complex architectural facade lighting system, our installation team has the expertise and equipment to execute flawlessly.

  • Site preparation and coordination
  • Safe rigging and mounting
  • Electrical wiring and connections
  • Testing and final commissioning
04 — Automation Integration

Smart Lighting
Intelligent Control

We integrate cutting-edge automation systems including KNX protocol-based solutions that allow you to control every aspect of your lighting from a single interface. Schedule scenes, adjust brightness, and manage energy consumption — all with the touch of a button.

Our automation solutions work with leading control systems and can be integrated with your existing smart home or building management system for a truly connected experience.

  • KNX-certified automation systems
  • Scene programming and scheduling
  • Integration with BMS and smart home
  • Energy monitoring and optimization
05 — Repair & Replacement

Expert Repairs
Minimal Downtime

When your lighting systems need attention, our repair team responds quickly to diagnose and resolve issues. We carry a comprehensive inventory of parts and components for all the brands we represent, ensuring fast turnaround times.

From replacing individual LED drivers to rebuilding entire fixture assemblies, we handle repairs of all complexities while maintaining the original quality and finish.

  • On-site diagnostics and troubleshooting
  • Genuine replacement parts supply
  • Fixture restoration and refurbishment
  • Warranty claim processing
06 — Annual Maintenance Contracts

Proactive Care
Lasting Performance

Our Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) provide comprehensive, proactive care for your entire lighting and automation infrastructure. Regular preventive maintenance ensures optimal performance, extends fixture life, and prevents costly emergency repairs.

We offer flexible AMC packages tailored to your specific needs — from quarterly inspections and cleaning to full-service coverage with priority response times and dedicated account management.

  • Scheduled preventive maintenance visits
  • Fixture cleaning and inspection
  • System health monitoring
  • Priority emergency response
