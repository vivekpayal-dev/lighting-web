Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions we hear most often from our clients.

What types of projects do you handle?

We handle a wide range of projects including 5-star hotels, luxury resorts, premium residences, corporate offices, retail spaces, hospitals, religious institutions, and large-scale commercial developments. From bespoke chandeliers to complete outdoor and automation systems.

Do you offer custom-designed lighting solutions?

Yes, we specialize in bespoke lighting design and manufacturing. Our team works closely with architects and interior designers to create custom chandeliers, pendant lights, wall sconces, and other decorative fixtures tailored to each project's unique requirements.

Which brands do you represent?

We are authorized representatives of 9+ global brands including Neko Lighting, O/M Light (Portugal), Unilamp Thailand, K-LITE India, Clear Lighting, Abby Lighting, Nirvana Lighting, Lafit Lighting, Jung Switches, and PASOLiTE LED.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we offer complete end-to-end services including professional installation by our expert technicians. We handle everything from site survey and design to delivery, installation, testing, and commissioning.

Do you offer after-sales support and maintenance?

Absolutely. We provide comprehensive after-sales support including repair services, replacement parts, and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) to ensure your lighting systems remain in perfect condition for years to come.

What is the typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical commercial installation may take 4-8 weeks from design approval to commissioning. Custom manufacturing projects may require additional time. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.
